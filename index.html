Welcome To The Chief Minister Health Endowment Fund, Gilgit-Baltistan

Our mission is to build a healthier, stronger community by ensuring that quality medical care is available to every citizen in need.

The Chief Minister Health Endowment Fund (CMHEF) ensures financial assistance for critical healthcare services in Gilgit-Baltistan, providing life-saving treatments, specialized procedures, and emergency care to those in need.
